按定义建图计算

```python
n,m=map(int,input().split())
D=[[0]*n for _ in range(n)]
A=[[0]*n for _ in range(n)]
for _ in range(m):
    a,b=map(int,input().split())
    D[a][a]+=1
    D[b][b]+=1
    A[a][b]=1
    A[b][a]=1
for lineD,lineA in zip(D,A):
    line=[]
    for i in range(n):
        line.append(lineD[i]-lineA[i])
    print(*line)
```
